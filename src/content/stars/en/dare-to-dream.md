Dare to dream, and let dreams be goals. Plan every step in their road, count every rock you'll face and build all the tools you need.

Power yourself with hope and will, love every inch of yourself and be your only one.

Stop every flow and be a standing tree inside a river…

But please — by all these circumstances — don't forget to take the first step.
