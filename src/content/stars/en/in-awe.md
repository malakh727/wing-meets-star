Maybe these are the white flowers that I used to love 🌼

The purity of its petals, the love of its art

Yes you're your own kind of art

And that what leaves me in awe

What if I had passion for that awe

What if I lived for myself,

Giving from the bottom of the pounding heart

From the depth of my feelings

Then will the world not be in war?

Will my war go down? So every human being will do what he lived for?

And that what leaves me in awe
