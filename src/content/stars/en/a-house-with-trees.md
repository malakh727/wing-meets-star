A house with trees as neighbors, birds sipping coffee with you, stars talking to you at night with their birth stories from nebulae and the scary deaths of their relatives. They say "we saw our dead relatives' ghosts, and some of them came back alive after a pause of time"…

And I believe them — as a human being who hasn't seen anyone for a long while.

Some live in the thoughts and some are saved in the heart, some have pulled me into their world with a blink of an eye…

Me — the body — refuses it as a whole and makes me sleepy after a long conversation about memories and feelings…

And I sleep in the cage of sleep, slowly falling and then deeply…

When you touch the ground of sleeping you'll find the key, the savior, and the escape.

Dreams make you live all that you remember and imagine, all that you've lived — and maybe what you will.

---

A house between stars can be perfect enough. The far star I loved will see me every day in my best conditions, and the moon will be my caring, changeable friend and I will be with it…

I'll go discover the birth factory and get emotional watching stars being born. I'll dive into one of the mysterious worlds — black holes — I'll see all the funny worlds in repeat and laugh at my mistakes…

But when I get deep in this dream it starts to disappear, and I know then that I have to go home.

With a soft sound beside my ear my lovely birds wake me up, and the ray of the sun comes whispering: *"the day has come — but I saw it all."*
