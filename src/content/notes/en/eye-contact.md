Sparkles all over the place. Is it just me? Or did his eyes hold enamored types of passion and mystery?

For a second, when our eyes met, that is what I thought.

But now he's back with the calm appearance and the lonely expression.

Eyes hold so much information about one's feelings. It became a habit for me to look directly into someone's eyes when talking to them — to catch a glimpse of their true feelings, like catching them lying or acting.

Revealing the truth gives you more options on how to act with them, which I find mind-blowing.
